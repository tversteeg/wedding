☑️ Checklist

2022-11-20  1 minute read

ASAP

  1. Get a passport if you don’t have one and ensure that it doesn’t expire if you do.
  2. Arrange your visa.
  3. Tell us your allergies if you have any.
  4. Send us your sizes if you’ll wear an Ao Dai.
  5. Change your bank debit card to international at your bank.
  6. Get an international drivers license.

When leaving

  1. Get around $100 dollars.
  2. Print your e-visa.
  3. Don’t forget your passport.

Smart to bring

  1. Mosquito repellant.
  2. Not too many warm clothes.
  3. Ear plugs.
